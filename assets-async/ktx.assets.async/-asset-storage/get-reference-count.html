fun <reified T> getReferenceCount(path: String): Int

Returns the amount of references to the asset under the given path of T type.

References include manual registration of the asset with add, scheduling the asset for loading with load, loadAsync or loadSync, and the amount of times the asset was referenced as a dependency of other assets.
